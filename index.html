Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To increase and diffuse the knowledge of athletics, improve sports innovation and academic preparedness and to inspire a positive social revolution in Foya Statutory District.

Vision

To make Foya an interactive community, a place to live, work, play and to foster an environment of productive young people.

Motto

"Building independent and creative thinkers, with purpose."
