About FODASU

Learn about our mission, vision, and the team driving positive change in Foya District

Who We Are

Foya District Academics Sports Union (FODASU) is a social-based, non-governmental and non-political organization established in Foya Statutory District that works with individuals and bodies of organizations to increase and diffuse the knowledge of athletics, improve sports innovation and academic preparedness, and to inspire a positive social revolution in Liberia.

Our Mission

To increase and diffuse the knowledge of athletics, improve sports innovation and academic preparedness and to inspire a positive social revolution in Foya Statutory District.

Our Vision

To make Foya an interactive community, a place to live, work, play and to foster an environment of productive young people.

Our Motto

"Building independent and creative thinkers, with purpose."
